Radar is full-stack location infrastructure for every product and service.

Our [Geofencing Platform](/documentation/geofencing/overview) is the industry-leading geofencing and location tracking platform, including geofencing, place visit detection, trip tracking, fraud detection, analytics, and more.

Our [Maps Platform](/documentation/maps/overview) is a cost-effective solution for geocoding APIs, search APIs, routing APIs, and base maps.

You can use our [SDKs](/sdk) and [APIs](/api) to build a wide range of use cases, including pickup and delivery tracking, location-based messaging, location verification, store locators, address autocomplete, and more.

Start exploring our building blocks and developer tools below.
