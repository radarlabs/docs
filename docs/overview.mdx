Radar is location infrastructure for every product and service. Developers use our hyper-accurate location tracking and geofencing SDKs and cost-effective geocoding APIs to power location-based experiences across hundreds of millions of devices worldwide.

## Quickstart

Want to get started quickly? Follow these three steps:

1. **Create geofences.** Create custom [geofences](/geofences) or enable out-of-the-box geofences for [Places](/places) (place, chain, and category detection) or [Regions](/regions) (country, state, DMA, and postal code detection).
2. **Integrate the Radar SDK or API into your app or website.** Integrate the [SDK](/sdk) or [API](/api). Or, before integrating, test geofencing with our [toolkit apps](/toolkit) for iOS and Android.
3. **Enable integrations.** Create a [webhook](/integrations#webhooks) or enable server-side [integrations](/integrations) to send Radar events and user context to other systems.

- **[Grocery](https://radar.com/solutions/grocery)**: Seamless pickup experiences, in-store app experiences, and store locators
- **[Travel](https://radar.com/solutions/travel)**: On-trip experienecs, privacy-first attribution, and nearby search
