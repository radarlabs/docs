Radar is the leading geofencing and location tracking platform. You can use our [SDKs](/sdk) and [APIs](/api) to build a wide range of location-based product and service experiences, including pickup and delivery tracking, location-triggered notifications, location verification, store locators, address autocomplete, and more.

## Quickstart

Want to get started quickly? Follow these three steps:

1. **Configure geofencing.** Create custom [geofences](/geofences) or enable out-of-the-box geofences for [Places](/places) (place, chain, and category detection) or [Regions](/regions) (country, state, DMA, and postal code detection).

2. **Integrate the Radar SDK or API into your app or website.** Integrate the [SDK](/sdk) or [API](/api). Or, before integrating, test geofencing with our [toolkit apps](/toolkit) for iOS and Android.

3. **Enable integrations.** Create a [webhook](/integrations#webhooks) or enable server-side [integrations](/integrations) to send Radar events and user context to other systems.

## Geofencing

Geofencing turns location data into context and events to help you build contextual app experiences. Go beyond on-device limits with support for unlimited geofences, polygon geofences, isochrone geofences, out-of-the-box geofences for places and regions, and more.
